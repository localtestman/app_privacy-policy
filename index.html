NetVista - Privacy Policy & Terms

Last Updated: May 2024

Welcome to NetVista. Your privacy is critically important to us. This document explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Location Information: We require access to your device's location (Foreground & Background) to identify cellular towers and map signal strength accurately.
  • Device Information: We collect device model, manufacturer, and Android ID for security, compatibility, and app performance monitoring.
  • Network & Signal Data: We monitor signal strength (RSRP, RSRQ), network type (4G, 5G), Cell ID, and Band information to provide core features.
  • User Account Data: If you sign in, we collect your email address via Firebase Authentication.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Display real-time network signal strength.
  • Provide a map view of nearby network towers.
  • Analyze and improve network performance for all users.

3. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services which may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google Play Services: Core Android functionality.
  • Firebase: For authentication and analytics.
  • AdMob: To display advertisements.
  • OSMDroid: To provide map services.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

You can request the deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at the email address provided below. We will process your request within a reasonable timeframe.

5. Terms and Conditions

By using NetVista, you agree to:

  • Grant necessary permissions (Location, Phone State) for the app to function.
  • Not use the app for any illegal or unauthorized activities.
  • Accept that signal data accuracy depends on your device hardware and provider.
